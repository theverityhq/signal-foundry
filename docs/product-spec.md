# Product Spec

## Name

Signal Foundry

## One-line summary

Audit local business websites for machine readability, structured data quality, and AI/search visibility readiness.

## Problem

Many local business websites look acceptable to humans but are weak for search and AI interpretation. Missing or incomplete structured data makes it harder for machines to understand:

- business identity
- hours
- services
- products
- menu information
- contact details
- service area

This creates a simple technical service opportunity: audit the site, explain the gaps, and fix the schema layer.

## Target customer

Small to medium local businesses within driving distance, especially:

- restaurants
- dentists
- auto repair shops
- opticians
- barbers
- landscapers
- home service businesses

## Core promise

We help local businesses become easier for search engines and AI systems to understand by cleaning up the machine-readable layer of their website.

## What this product should do

### MVP

1. Import a CSV of business prospects.
2. Visit core pages for each business.
3. Detect JSON-LD schema types.
4. Score the site for AI/search visibility readiness.
5. Output a report with missing signals and recommended schema type.

### V2

1. Discover businesses by radius and category.
2. Add robots/crawlability checks.
3. Add microdata and RDFa extraction.
4. Generate richer JSON-LD recommendations by industry.
5. Produce outreach-ready audit summaries.

## Success criteria

- Audit 25-50 websites in one run.
- Produce reports clear enough to use in outreach.
- Identify obvious gaps quickly and defensibly.
- Create a repeatable offer that can lead into paid schema fixes.

## Pricing model

- Audit: $99-$149
- Schema fix/install: $250-$500
- Annual maintenance: $100-$200/year

## What not to promise

- guaranteed ranking improvements
- guaranteed ChatGPT placement
- guaranteed menu rendering in AI products
- guaranteed lead growth

## What to promise

- better machine readability
- cleaner structured data
- stronger search/AI readiness
- clearer business entity signals
- a concrete before/after technical improvement
